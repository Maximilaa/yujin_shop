Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our eco-friendly straw bags

What is the size of the bag?

+

Our standard straw bags measure approximately 35cm x 25cm x 15cm (L x W x H). We also offer mini versions (20cm x 15cm x 10cm) and large totes (45cm x 35cm x 20cm) to suit different needs.

How to Order

+

Simply browse our collection, select your preferred bag, choose the size and color, then add to cart. We accept all major credit cards and PayPal. Orders are processed within 24 hours.

Shipping Method

+

We offer free standard shipping (5-7 business days) on orders over $50. Express shipping (2-3 business days) is available for $9.99. International shipping is available to most countries.

How do you take care of a rattan bag?

+

Keep your bag dry and avoid prolonged exposure to direct sunlight. Clean with a soft, damp cloth and mild soap if needed. Store in a cool, dry place. With proper care, your bag will last for years.

Is the bag material water-resistant?

+

Our straw bags have natural water resistance but are not completely waterproof. They can handle light rain and humidity, but we recommend avoiding heavy downpours. For extra protection, we offer water-resistant linings as an add-on.
