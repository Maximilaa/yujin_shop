Our Story

Yujin was founded with a simple mission: to create beautiful, functional bags that don't harm our planet. We work directly with local artisans who specialize in traditional straw-weaving techniques.

Each of our products is crafted using sustainable materials harvested responsibly from renewable sources. We're committed to fair trade practices and supporting rural communities through ethical employment.
